---

## **🧠 Intelligence Tab - What It Does**

The **Intelligence Service** is an **AI-powered account intelligence feature** that provides executives with strategic insights, market data, and competitive intelligence about their client accounts.

---

## **🎯 Purpose & Objectives**

**Main Goal:** Transform raw account data into **actionable strategic intelligence** for account managers and executives.

Instead of just showing KPIs (revenue, projects, allocations), the Intelligence tab provides:
- **Strategic context** about what's happening with the client
- **Market intelligence** about the client's industry and competitors
- **Executive insights** from news and public data
- **AI-powered answers** to specific questions about accounts

---

## **🔧 Key Features (Based on Git History)**

### **1. Intelligence Feed** (`GET /{account_id}/feed`)
- **What:** A news/updates feed for each account
- **Contains:** Latest developments, market trends, industry news relevant to the client
- **Purpose:** Keep account teams informed about what's happening with their clients

### **2. Strategic Priorities** (`GET /{account_id}/priorities`)
- **What:** AI-extracted strategic priorities for the client
- **Contains:** Key business objectives, initiatives, focus areas
- **Purpose:** Align delivery with client's strategic goals

### **3. Competitive Landscape** (`GET /{account_id}/competitors`)
- **What:** Competitor analysis for the client
- **Contains:** Who competes with the client, market positioning
- **Purpose:** Understand client's competitive environment

### **4. AI Query Engine** (`POST /{account_id}/queries`)
- **What:** Ask questions about an account and get AI-generated answers
- **Example Questions:** 
  - "What are the key risks for this client?"
  - "What opportunities should we pursue?"
  - "What's their recent market performance?"
- **Purpose:** On-demand intelligence generation

### **5. Market Data Integration**
Based on the commit `a48753f`, this includes:
- **Stock quotes** (for publicly traded clients)
- **Company news** (executive movements, announcements)
- **Executive insights** (sentiment about leadership)
- **Sentiment analysis** (market sentiment toward the client)

### **6. Internal Market Intelligence**
From commit `4b21bf7` and `ab448aa`:
- Integration with **Draup reports** (market research platform)
- Internal market intelligence data
- RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) for document analysis

---

## **🏗️ Technical Architecture**

### **Components:**

1. **Intelligence Router** (`app/routers/intelligence.py`)
   - FastAPI endpoints for the Intelligence tab
   - Handles HTTP requests from the frontend

2. **Intelligence Service** (`app/services/intelligence_service.py`)
   - Business logic layer
   - Orchestrates data gathering and AI processing

3. **AI Agents** (`app/agents/`)
   - **Orchestrator**: Coordinates multiple AI agents
   - **Account Summarization Agent**: Generates account summaries
   - **WSR Agent**: Analyzes Weekly Status Reports
   - **Business Line Agent**: Industry-specific insights
   - **Revenue Analysis Agent**: Financial insights

4. **Market Data Providers** (`app/market_data/`)
   - **AlphaVantage**: Stock market data API
   - **Finnhub**: Financial news and data
   - **Executive Analysis**: Extract executive insights
   - **Sentiment Analysis**: Determine market sentiment

5. **Knowledge Base Service** (`bedrock_kb_service`)
   - AWS Bedrock for AI/LLM capabilities
   - Document retrieval and RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)

6. **Data Access Layer** (`app/dao/`)
   - DAOs (Data Access Objects) for querying the database
   - Connect intelligence service to trusted_zone KPIs

## **🔄 How It Works (Technical Flow)**

```
User Request (Intelligence Tab)
   ↓
FastAPI Router (/intelligence)
   ↓
Intelligence Service
   ↓
   ├─→ [1] Fetch Account Data (from trusted_zone)
   ├─→ [2] Call Market Data APIs (stocks, news, sentiment)
   ├─→ [3] Query Knowledge Base (internal documents, WSRs)
   ├─→ [4] Run AI Agents (summarization, analysis)
   ├─→ [5] Orchestrate Multi-Agent Workflow
   ↓
Aggregate & Format Response
   ↓
Return Intelligence Feed/Priorities/Insights
   ↓
Display in Dashboard UI
```
